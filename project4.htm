Project 4: Surreal Landscape

Step into a dreamlike world where strange things happen. A giant fish floats in the sky, and a red telephone booth stands on a hill like it’s from another time. An inflatable swan glides in a small pond, surrounded by old computers and a stone face. The pinkish-purple color makes the scene feel both futuristic and familiar. Each object has its own story, and you’ll be curious about everything.
