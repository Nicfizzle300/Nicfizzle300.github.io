Project 1: Contour Line Illustration

The image depicts a meticulously crafted arrangement of rocks, each meticulously shaded to achieve depth and texture. The range of tones imbues the composition with a natural and organic feel, evoking the serenity of a serene landscape. The harmonious interplay between light and shadow elevates the visual appeal, creating a dynamic and lifelike impression. The delicate linework adds to the intricate drawing, accentuating the rugged yet harmonious structure of the stones. Ultimately, the image captures a sense of quiet balance, emphasizing the beauty found in simple, natural forms.
